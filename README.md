# Solo CA Office CRM

## Manage Your CA Practice from One Dashboard

**Solo CA Office CRM** is a powerful **PHP & MySQL practice management system** designed for independent Chartered Accountants, tax consultants, accounting professionals, and small CA firms.

Manage clients, GST, income tax returns, TDS, ROC compliance, notices, appointments, tasks, invoices, documents, communications, and secure client portal access from one modern dashboard.

Built as a **self-hosted solution**, the application gives you complete ownership of your software, database, files, client records, and business information with a **one-time purchase** and **lifetime ownership**.

Run your entire accounting and tax consultancy practice with one complete, secure, and professional office management solution.
